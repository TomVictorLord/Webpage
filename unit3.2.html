3.2 Describe ways to improve productivity and efficiency

Trello is a good way to improve productivity as you can write notes to remind you of things you might later forget like button shortcuts or specific bits of code.
Another way to improve productivity would be to ask the people around you who you know are good at what you're struggling in rather than the teacher who might be helping someone else.
Do your own learning, look things up on Google, watch tutorials on YouTube.
There is plenty of free stuff to help you.
